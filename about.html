Who we are

Infrastructure for
Financial Sovereignty.

MINT is a modern private wealth platform built on alignment and fair access. We exist to redefine how ambitious individuals grow, manage and leverage capital.

"At our core, we believe wealth management should be performance-driven, not product-driven. That is why we operate on a performance-only fee model. We earn when you earn."

Through curated OpenStrategies, real-time tracking tools, portfolio-backed lending and seamless capital mobility, MINT delivers more than investment access. We provide financial architecture.

As we expand, MINT will continue to broaden access across asset classes, including curated private market and art exposure, within a disciplined and structured ecosystem.

MINT is not just an investment platform. It is a capital infrastructure for modern wealth on the go.
