Who We Are

Building for modern
Financial Sovereignty.

MINT is a modern private wealth platform built on alignment and fair access. We exist to redefine how ambitious individuals grow, manage and leverage capital.

"At our core, we believe wealth management should be performance-driven, not product-driven. That is why we operate on a performance-only fee model. We earn when you earn. No hidden incentives. No unnecessary complexity."

We combine disciplined investment strategies, transparent data and intelligent technology to provide structured access across asset classes, including public markets, private opportunities and alternative investments, without the traditional opacity of asset management.

Through curated OpenStrategies, real-time tracking tools, portfolio-backed lending and seamless capital mobility, MINT delivers more than investment access. We provide financial architecture. A system designed to help you grow capital, access liquidity when needed, allocate into private and alternative opportunities, and build long-term financial sovereignty.

MINT is not just an investment platform. It is a capital infrastructure for modern wealth on the go.
