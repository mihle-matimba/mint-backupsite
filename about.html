Who We Are

MINT is a modern private wealth platform built on alignment and fair access.

MINT exists to redefine how ambitious individuals grow, manage and leverage capital. We combine disciplined investment strategies, transparent data and intelligent technology to provide structured access across asset classes, including public markets, private opportunities and alternative investments, — without the traditional opacity of asset management.

At our core, we believe wealth management should be performance-driven, not product-driven. That’s why we operate on a performance-only fee model — we earn when you earn. No hidden incentives. No unnecessary complexity.

Through curated OpenStrategies, real-time tracking tools, and portfolio-backed lending,and seamless capital mobility, MINT delivers more than investment access. We provide financial architecture. A system designed to help you grow capital, access liquidity when needed, allocate into private and alternative opportunities, and build long-term financial sovereignty.

As we expand, MINT will continue to broaden access across asset classes, including curated private market and art exposure, within a disciplined and structured ecosystem.

MINT is not just an investment platform. It is a capital infrastructure for modern wealth on the go.
