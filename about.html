Who We Are

Building for
Sovereignty.

"At our core, we believe wealth management should be performance-driven, not product-driven. That is why we operate on a performance-only fee model. We earn when you earn. No hidden incentives. No unnecessary complexity."

MINT is a modern private wealth platform built on alignment and fair access. We exist to redefine how ambitious individuals grow, manage and leverage capital.

Through curated OpenStrategies, real-time tracking tools, portfolio-backed lending and seamless capital mobility, MINT delivers more than investment access. We provide financial architecture. A system designed to help you grow capital, access liquidity when needed, and build long-term financial sovereignty.

MINT is not just an investment platform. It is a capital infrastructure for modern wealth on the go.
